About

EZ Pi is a simple and easy to use(as the name suggests) interface to make interaction between you and your Raspberry Pi easy. Complex and slightly annoying processes like installing a server and all of it's dependencies or switching on GPIOs are made very easy for you, whether you are a first time user of the RPi or someone who has been using one for years. EZ Pi will make your prototyping process easier than ever before. For more information, visit EZ Pi here. Happy prototyping :)
